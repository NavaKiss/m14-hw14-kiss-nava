Nice to meet you! I’m a photographer, digital strategist, and social media manager currently based out of Oceanside, California. I’ve been a lover of the outdoors and ocean life as long as I can remember, and I take my camera absolutely everywhere.

Nearly all my free time is spent outdoors, being active in some capacity, whether that's hiking, camping, SCUBA diving, horseback riding, running, or attempting to surf. I find myself a perpetual beginner, and my photography career started as a creative outlet I used to document my adventures.

Now, it's part of how I help my company (and freelance clients) capture the magic of different events, facilities, and activities!
